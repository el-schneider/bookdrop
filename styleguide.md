# Bookdrop Styleguide

## Direction

Bookdrop uses a calm, monochrome, Swiss/editorial interface. The product is private book delivery, not productivity software. The interface should feel quiet, exact, and durable: a small library desk, not a dashboard template.

## Typography

- **Newsreader** is used only for major page headlines and large editorial statements.
- **Fragment Mono** is used for all UI: body copy, labels, navigation, buttons, tables, forms, code, and helper text.
- Use few sizes. Build hierarchy with whitespace, rules, alignment, weight, and measure before increasing type size.
- Body copy stays compact and readable. Large headlines get room to breathe.
- Avoid heavy weights. Prefer regular and medium.

## Color

- Monochrome only.
- Light mode: warm paper background, black ink, quiet gray rules.
- Dark mode: near-black background, soft white ink, restrained gray rules.
- No colorful success/error palettes. Status is expressed through text, borders, and subtle tone.
- Links and controls do not use accent colors.

## Shape and line

- No rounded corners.
- No shadows.
- Use thin 1px rules as the main visual structure.
- Borders should feel measured and architectural, not decorative.
- Dashed rules are acceptable for drop targets and separators.

## Layout

- Prefer clear grids and strong left alignment.
- Use generous negative space, especially around headlines and section changes.
- Cards are simple ruled panels, not floating surfaces.
- Tables are calm and dense enough to scan. Use horizontal rules, not zebra striping.

## Interaction

- Interactions are quiet: underline, inverted monochrome, or border contrast.
- Focus states must be visible and monochrome.
- Dark mode toggle is explicit, small, and persistent.
- Upload progress is functional and linear, without animation flourishes.

## Voice

- Copy is direct, inviting, and book-centered.
- Avoid SaaS hype: no “supercharge”, “seamless”, “delight”.
- Use words like shelf, private, send, reader, sync, EPUB, Kobo.

## Anti-patterns

- Rounded cards/buttons/inputs.
- Shadows, gradients, colorful badges, pill statuses.
- Many type sizes.
- Generic Laravel/Breeze styling.
- Decorative motion or flashy hover effects.
